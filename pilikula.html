    Pilikula Nisargadhama is a popular eco-tourism and recreational park located near Mangalore, Karnataka, India. Here are some key details about it:

1. **Location**: Pilikula Nisargadhama is situated around 12 kilometers from Mangalore city center, in the southern part of Karnataka.
            
2. **Features**:
               - **Zoo**: The park has a well-maintained zoo that houses a variety of animals and birds, including tigers, lions, and various species of deer.
               - **Botanical Garden**: It features a botanical garden with a range of flora, providing a serene environment for nature lovers.
               - **Water Park**: There’s a water park within the premises, offering a range of fun activities for families and children.
               - **Science Center**: The park includes a science center with interactive exhibits, making it educational and entertaining.
               - **Heritage Village**: This area showcases traditional Karnataka architecture and culture, giving visitors a glimpse into the region's history and heritage.
               - **Lake**: Pilikula also has a picturesque lake where visitors can enjoy boating.
            
3. **Activities**: The park offers various activities such as nature walks, educational tours, and recreational facilities, making it a great destination for both relaxation and learning.
            
4. **Entry Fees and Timings**: Entry fees and timings can vary, so it’s a good idea to check the official website or contact them for the most current information before planning your visit.
            
5. **Conservation Efforts**: The park is also involved in various conservation efforts, including the protection of wildlife and promotion of environmental awareness.
            
Pilikula Nisargadhama is a great place to explore nature, learn about local wildlife, and enjoy some family-friendly activities.
